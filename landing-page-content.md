## 9. Why Trust Ayesha With This Decision?
Ayesha Saleem is the Founder and CEO of UnizConnect, a British Council Certified Counsellor, and a study abroad advisor.
But her story is not just professional. It is personal.
Around 12 years ago, Ayesha got into Chemical Engineering at UET Lahore on merit. At the time, it felt like a major achievement.
But by the end of her second year, she realised the core engineering courses did not excite her. She was putting in the effort, but still getting B and C grades.
At the same time, she was thriving in non-technical electives, societies, creative writing, and communication-focused roles.
She won awards for prose writing and was named Best Presenter in her batch.
That is when she realised something many students realise too late:
*Being capable does not always mean you are on the right path.*
So, she entered the international education field.
Over the years, she was promoted several times, travelled nationally and internationally, worked with students and education leaders, and found work that genuinely energised her.
Looking back, she wishes she had a coach or mentor while graduating. Someone who could have helped her understand her strengths earlier.
That is exactly why she founded UnizConnect: to help students avoid years of confusion and make better education decisions before it is too late.

   Yes! Ayesha will guide you on scholarship suitability and chances based on your profile.
4. **My CGPA is low. Should I still book?**
   Absolutely. A low CGPA affects strategy, not all opportunities. The session will clarify what’s realistic and how to improve your chances.
5. **Is this session only for UK admissions?**
   No. You can discuss options for the UK, US, Malaysia, Europe, Turkey, New Zealand, and other destinations. The focus is finding the best fit for your profile.
6. **Can I book this session for career guidance?**
   Yes. It’s designed to align your study abroad choices with long-term career goals.
7. **Will I get a written summary?**
   Yes. After the session, you will receive a custom recap email covering key advice and next steps.
8. **Do I need to upload my CV?**
   Yes. Your CV helps Ayesha assess your profile and prepare for a personalized session.
9. **Is admission or scholarship guaranteed?**
   No. The session provides strategy, clarity, and guidance, but does not guarantee results.
10. **How will I get the session link?**
    After payment verification, the team will send you Ayesha’s calendar link via email and WhatsApp.
11. **Which study level is this session for?**
    This session is for Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Research-level students planning to study abroad.
